## 8. Symptoms Section ✅

### Updated Symptoms:

1. **Chronic Joint & Back Pain**
   - Pain in knees, neck, or lower back
   - Worse with activity, better with rest

2. **Morning Stiffness & Rigidity**
   - "Locked" feeling in joints
   - Most severe in morning

3. **Grating Sensation (Crepitus)**
   - Crunching/crackling sound
   - Indicates cartilage wear

4. **Numbness & Tingling**
   - Pins and needles sensation
   - Nerve compression in spine

5. **Loss of Flexibility**
   - Reduced range of motion
   - Difficulty with daily movements

6. **Joint Swelling & Weakness**
   - Soft swelling or bony growths
   - Muscle weakness around joints
