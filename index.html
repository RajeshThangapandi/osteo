Symptoms of Osteoarthritis & Spondylosis

Do you experience any of these signs?

Chronic Neck/Back Pain

Persistent pain in the neck or lower back that worsens with activity or prolonged sitting.

Stiffness & Limited Mobility

Difficulty moving your neck or bending your back, especially in the morning.

Radiating Pain

Pain that travels down your arms (cervical) or legs (lumbar), often with tingling.

Numbness & Weakness

Loss of sensation or muscle weakness in arms, hands, legs, or feet.

Headaches & Dizziness

Frequent headaches originating from the neck or balance issues (cervical).

Grinding Sensation

Crepitus or grinding feeling when moving your neck or back.

Recognize these symptoms? Don't delay diagnosis.
